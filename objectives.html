Objectives



The college has the following objectives:
  • To provide R & D consultancy which will earn some revenue to the college, promote contract and be of service to the govt./civil organization and industries.
  • To function in a decentralized way and to have respect& tolerance for the views of every individuals.
  • To sharpen the cutting edge of knowledge from time to time.
  • To achieve academic integrity and accountability.
  • To address issues of national relevance as well as global concern and to have sensitivity to social responsibilities.
