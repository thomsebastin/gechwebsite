Rules & Regulations


          Once the acceptance of application closes , No application would be submitted later in any case.

          It is mandatory for the students who have applied for a particular company to appear at the Pre-Placement talk of the company. If the student wishes not to appear for the company after the presentation, he/she has to give it in written, along with the reason for it to the placement coordinator for the particular company. It is mandatory for the students shortlisted by the company on merit/CV basis to appear for the first phase of Recruitment Process.No application acceptable in this case. If the student fails to appear for the presentation or doesn’t appear for first phase of recruitment process without giving written application , the User ID of the student will be blocked for 2 placement weeks(in which placement is taking place) and wouldn’t be allowed to sit for any company in the same period.

          The following misconducts from the student will attract penalization as per the Disciplinary Action given below the student withdraws from any phase of Recruitment Process without being eliminated by the company .

The student misbehaves in the interview as:

  • Tells the company that he/she doesn’t want to join the company.
  • Speaks negatively about the company or college.
  • Lays down the condition to join the company (if not asked).
  • The student talks to the Company Executive directly at any time other than Pre-Placement Talks.
  • The student asks any irrelevant questions or behaves in an unacceptable way during the Pre-Placement Talks.
  • Misbehavior with placement team will result in debarment.
  • The student found breaching any of these rules would attract a minimum punishment as per Rule-2 which could be increased as per the discretion of Head, Department of Training & Placement.

          The student has to be present at the venue of PPT/Written Test/GD/Case Study/Interview 30 minutes before the time allotted. No student would be allowed in any case to appear for process after the allotted time. Otherwise further action will be taken according to Rule-2.

          It is mandatory for the student to be in formal dress throughout the Recruitment Process (including PPT/written test). No student would be allowed for the PPT/Recruitment Process if he/she is not in formal dress. Otherwise further action will be taken according to Rule-2.

          Any student found involved in ragging or any indisciplinary activity will be debarred for the entire placement session.
